Catharsis - Working to repair relationships

A mobile app that fosters genuine conversations by teaching communication skills. It provides users with practical exercises and real-time feedback to enhance their interactions.

Dartpop - Thrifting for students

A secondhand e-commerce app for Dartmouth students to list and buy items (ranging from clothes to textbooks) from each other, allowing them to update their wardrobes and get rid of clothes they no longer wear during the term.

Biblio - Community book exchange

A dynamic platform that allows users to trade, discover, and connect over books. It helps users save money, encourage more reading, reduce overconsumption, and build a community of book lovers.

Mahjouba Initiative - Reviving the Moroccan craft sector (Work in progress)

A mobile app facilitating the construction of various motorbike parts by local Moroccan craftsmen. Users can search, accept, and submit their part to the atelier, Mahjouba Initiative.
